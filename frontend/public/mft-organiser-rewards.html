You choose your organiser percentage when creating a battle. When the battle ends, your percentage of the total prize pool goes straight to your wallet. Simple, predictable, automatic.

Example: 20 traders × $50 entry = $1,000 pool. You take 10% = $100 yours.

15–50%

When you join your own battle, the prize pool size determines how much of your entry fee you get back. Bigger battle = bigger rebate tier. This rebate comes from the winner's prize pool.

Example: $50 entry, $1,200 prize pool (30% tier) = $15 back to you.

Volume Cash Bonus

$100–$10K

Totally separate from commissions and rebates. If your battles generate $10,000+ in total prize pool volume in any 30-day window, you earn an extra cash bonus on top of everything else.

Example: $10,000 monthly volume = +$100 cash bonus.
